Event Overview

Duration & Venue

  • 48 Hours (2 Days, 2 Nights)
  • Anna Auditorium, VIT Vellore
  • Dates: During Gravitas 2025

Organizers

  • BIS Standards Club, VIT Vellore
  • In Association with Bureau of Indian Standards, Chennai
  • Part of Gravitas 2025

Participation

  • Open to all VIT students
  • Team Size: 3-5 members
  • Registration via VTOP
  • Form teams before registration

Objectives

  • Promote awareness of Indian Standards
  • Foster interdisciplinary teamwork
  • Encourage tech-based compliance solutions
  • Focus on safety and sustainability

Innovation Tracks

Smart Infrastructure & Urban Tech

Build smart, compliant infrastructure tools using BIS civil/structural codes like IS 875 & IS 16700.

Healthcare & Biomedical Standards

Develop medical devices, sanitation & hygiene tech using standards like IS 13450, IS 15700.

Environmental Sustainability

Create innovations around water, air, waste, and green systems aligned with IS 10500, IS 5182.

Consumer Safety & Product Quality

Verify labels, test product safety, or create alert systems using standards like IS 15700.

Energy & Electrification

Battery, LED, solar system innovations with standards like IS 16046 and IS 16102.

Digital Systems & Cybersecurity

AI ethics, secure systems & data compliance based on IS 17428 & ISO/IEC standards.

Mechanical Systems & Manufacturing

Innovations in manufacturing, design, and automation based on IS 210, IS 2062, IS 10218.

Open Innovation (Wildcard)

Impactful ideas that don't fit a single domain but align with BIS principles.
